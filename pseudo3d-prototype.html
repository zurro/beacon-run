What To Judge

  • Does the road curvature immediately create more tension than the current flat scroll?
  • Do the bigger blockers and pickups feel anchored to the road instead of floating on the screen?
  • Does the horizon and elevation add enough excitement to justify a larger systems rewrite?
  • Does lane switching still feel readable when the path bends underneath the player?

Prototype Scope

This page is intentionally separate from the main runner. It is a visual decision tool, not a production migration. If the feeling lands, the real work would be moving gameplay from screen-space movement into segment-space projection.

Projected track segments, bigger projected collectibles, larger blockers, wasteland sky and dust, current runner sprite, no full collision or scoring rewrite yet.
