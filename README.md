A **3-lane infinite runner** based on the universe of Cobalt Strike.

## Controls:
  - **Left/Right**: change lanes
  - **Up**: jump (avoid tall barriers)
  - **Down**: slide (avoid low barriers)
  - **Space**: pause
  - Mobile: swipe left/right/up/down

## Power-ups
- **SHIELD**: survive a hit
- **MAGNET**: pulls power-ups toward you
- **BOOST**: temporary speed boost

## Rewards
- **COIN** trails: +100 score per coin
- **BEACON** item: +500 score
